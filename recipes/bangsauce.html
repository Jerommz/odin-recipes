Bang bang sauce recipe

Best bang bang sauce recipe in the world!

This bang bang sauce is a sweet and spicy mayonnaise-based sauce that is good on fried fish, chicken, fried potatoes â€” almost everything!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons sweet Thai chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar

Step-By-Step

  1. Put all the ingredients in a bowl
  2. Mix them all together until smooth
